The Pull

A sight, a smile, a word,
The most captivating art,
A joke, a gaze, a laugh,
An uncertain pinch in the heart.

Who, what, how?
I want, I need more

A touch, a swirl, a step,
Individual to unity,
An embrace, a warmth, a scent,
A hint of eternity.

I see the novelty,
I will drink the tea

A caress, a nap, a trust,
The time it takes to feel,
A soft, a long, a given,
The disordered appeal,

I feel the pull, I know the way,
Not to harm, we have to play

A song, a proposal, a reason,
It was a light to be lit,
A dance, a longing, a kiss,
The place where only us fit,

The smile is true, the mystery is whole,
Who are you?

A little, a bit, a few,
I discover and I enjoy,
A load, a lot, a plenty,
The one was not coy

You light a way,
you show your own

A question, a desire, a fire,
Heart hops and maybe stops,
A fence, a dare, a shove,
A sense, a care, a love,

The last step in queue,
The final note rings true,
Have your thoughts been blue,
I think it may be our perks,
Now I only feel the fireworks
