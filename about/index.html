About

Alright I’m pretty sure I’m just doing yak-shaving. Anyway it’s about time this page gets updated.

This website is for two persons, that are tightly coupled. John Doe and Killian. John is more the kind of intellectual whereas Killian has things to say about emotions.

Killian does not sign his posts, but John does. (Killian likes puns more that John)

Sometimes, John wants to talk about a serious topic but knows he can’t really explain it well, or the time spent would not be worth it. Then he calls for Killian, and together they write. John still signs, but you recognize well the meddling of Killian. It’s easy, just count the amount of ‘therefore’, of ‘consequentially’ and ‘nevertheless’.

So here you are, I (as a dual entity) am very happy to present you the space of freedom I have. It’s really been a nice escape route for all these pent up feelings and reasonings.

Oh by the way.
You need to take off you shoes before entering.
And your socks as well.
And you won’t need your coat or your other clothes for that matter.
You won’t need your skin or your bones, you can leave them in the box as well.
Actually, just take your spirit and go on.

That’s the only way to properly enter the exit.
